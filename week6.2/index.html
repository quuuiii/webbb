Let me not to the marriage of true minds admit impediments. Love is not love which alters when it alteration finds, or bends with the remover to remove. O no! it is an ever-fixed mark. Whose worth's unknown, although his height be taken. Love's not Time's fool. Love alters not with his brief hours and weeks, but bears it out even to the edge of doom.
